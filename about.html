My Professional Journey

From Biomedical Engineering to Healthcare Leadership

Engineering solutions for Africa's healthcare challenges

Who I Am

As a Biomedical Engineer and healthcare entrepreneur, I've dedicated my career to bridging the gap between medical technology and practical healthcare delivery in emerging markets. My journey began at Vellore Institute of Technology, where I specialized in medical device innovation, focusing on cost-effective solutions for resource-limited settings.

Professional Evolution

After completing my B.Tech, I recognized the critical need for reliable medical infrastructure in Africa. This insight led to the founding of Lean Healthcare Solutions, where we've:

  • Established partnerships with 12+ global medical equipment manufacturers
  • Implemented diagnostic technologies in 50+ healthcare facilities
  • Reduced equipment downtime by 40% through innovative maintenance programs
